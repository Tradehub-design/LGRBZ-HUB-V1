# LGRBZ v1.5 Release Notes

## Summary

v1.5 expands the Business Suite for planning, cashflow and portfolio modelling.

## Added

- Retirement planner engine and workspace
- Cashflow planner engine and workspace
- Scenario simulator engine and workspace
- Net worth engine and workspace
- Goal engine upgrade
- Real goals page
- Position sizing engine and workspace
- FIRE calculator engine and workspace
- Business Model planning summary
- Dashboard planning commands
- Business Suite navigation links

## Known Limitations

- Planning assumptions are fixed defaults for now.
- Editable assumptions and saved scenarios are planned later.
