# LGRBZ v1.8 Release Notes

## Summary

v1.8 expands TradingView-style market intelligence and company research.

## Added

- Market watchlist registry
- Market Watchlist workspace
- Market Movers engine and workspace
- Market quote provider foundation
- Market Centre
- Price chart foundation
- Company Research workspace
- Company profile model
- Company metrics model
- Company news model and panel
- Stock Screener foundation
- Market Calendar foundation
- Technical indicator engine
- Technicals workspace
- Market summary engine
- Market intelligence navigation

## Known Limitations

- Market data uses demo provider values.
- Company profiles, metrics, news and calendar are demo data.
- Real provider integration is planned later.
