# LGRBZ v1.4 Release Notes

## Summary

v1.4 adds the first intelligence layer for LGRBZ.

## Added

- Portfolio intelligence engine
- AI Insights workspace
- Sector intelligence engine and workspace
- Country intelligence engine and workspace
- Income intelligence engine and workspace
- Currency intelligence engine and workspace
- Watchlist intelligence engine
- Intelligent watchlist ideas
- Research Lab workspace
- Research prompts
- Intelligence navigation links

## Known Limitations

- Insights are deterministic rule-based calculations.
- Live AI assistant is not connected yet.
- Watchlist ideas are rule-generated placeholders until live market data is connected.
